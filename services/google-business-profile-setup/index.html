Why Proper Google Business Profile Setup Is Critical

Most businesses create a Google Business Profile but never structure it correctly. Categories are wrong. Services are incomplete. Location signals are weak. This directly affects local rankings.

A properly optimized Google Business Profile becomes a powerful local ranking asset. When structured strategically, it increases relevance, authority, and trust signals inside Google’s local algorithm.
